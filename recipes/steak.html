Steak Recipe

[describe recipe]

Ingredients

  • 2 Thick cuts(0.75 - 1.5 inches) of steak of your choice (ribeye, new york strip, filet mignon)
  • 1 garlic head
  • salt & black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 2 table spoons of high smoke point cooking fat

Steps

  1. Pat steaks dry and season generously with course salt (avoid table salt).
  2. Set steaks on a wire rack and refrigerate steaks for 2-18 hours.
  3. Remove steaks from refrigerator and allow them to temper for 20 minutes.
  4. Preheat cast iron or stainless steel pan and add high smoke point cooking fat.
  5. Once fat reaches 400-450 degrees F (205-260 degrees C) carefully lay steaks onto the pan away from you.
  6. Press steaks firmly or use a food weight to ensure a good sear.
  7. Flip after 1 minute.
  8. Add freshly cracked black pepper and flip again after 1 minute.
  9. Add freshly cracked black pepper and flip again after 1 minute.
  10. Use an instant read thermometer to check the internal temperature. Once temperature reaches approximately 110 degrees F (43.5 degrees C) cut the top half of a garlic head and add cut-side down into pan.
  11. Lower heat and add butter. Use a spoon to baste butter over the steak.
  12. Once generously basted for 1 minute, place steaks on wooden cutting board to rest for 10 minutes.
  13. Cut and enjoy.
